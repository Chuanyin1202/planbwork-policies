Voxmemo App Support

Technical support and FAQ for AI Voice Memo app

About Voxmemo

Voxmemo is an intelligent voice memo application that allows you to quickly record todo items and expenses through voice. The app provides AI-powered smart categorization, time parsing, and local data storage.

Main Features

  • Voice Recording: Record voice memos and automatically convert to text
  • Smart Categorization: AI automatically identifies todo types (Event/Checklist)
  • Time Parsing: Automatically parses time and location information from voice
  • Expense Tracking: Quickly record expenses and income
  • Local Storage: All data securely stored on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

App can't record audio?

Please ensure you have granted Voxmemo microphone permissions:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Voxmemo
  • Android: Settings → Apps → Voxmemo → Permissions → Microphone

Speech-to-text is inaccurate?

Speech-to-text uses OpenAI Whisper API for processing. For best results:

  • Record in a quiet environment
  • Speak clearly at a moderate pace
  • Avoid excessive background noise

Todo categorization is wrong?

AI smart categorization is based on semantic analysis and may occasionally misclassify. You can:

  • Manually change the todo item type
  • Edit the todo content to more accurately express your intent

How to backup expense records?

Currently, Voxmemo stores all data locally on your device. Deleting the app will also delete all data, so please ensure you have completed necessary backups before deletion.

App crashes or runs slowly?

Please try the following solutions:

  • Restart the application
  • Check for app updates
  • Ensure your device has sufficient storage space
  • Restart your device

Privacy and Data Security

We highly value your privacy. All todo items and expense records are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to cloud servers. Audio files are only used for transcription and are deleted immediately after processing. For detailed information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
