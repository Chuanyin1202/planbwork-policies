Planbwork ("we", "us", or "our") highly values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Voxmemo application ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Collection Scope

1.1 Data You Provide

  • Todo items: Tasks you enter via voice or text input
  • Audio recordings: Voice content you record (used only for transcription)
  • Expense records: Income and expense data you input

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

We do not collect device identifiers, usage analytics, or crash reports.

Important: We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information. The App does not require account registration to use.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data Type Purpose Processing Method
Todo items Personal task management Stored in local SQLite database
Audio recordings Speech-to-text conversion Processed by a third-party cloud transcription service, then deleted
Todo text Smart categorization and normalization Processed by a third-party AI service
Expense records Personal finance tracking Stored in local SQLite database

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All todo items and expense records are stored in your device's SQLite database
  • Data is not automatically backed up to the cloud
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

3.2 Cloud Processing

Third-Party Cloud Processing:

  • Audio files are used only for transcription and deleted immediately after processing
  • Text content is used for smart categorization (time parsing, category detection) and is not permanently stored
  • Processing follows the provider's data handling policies

3.3 Security Measures

  • All network transmissions use HTTPS encryption
  • We apply reasonable technical measures to protect data

4. Data Sharing Policy

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Third-party AI services for speech-to-text and smart categorization

4.2 Legal Requirements

When required by law or legal process

4.3 Protecting Rights

To protect our or others' rights, property, or safety

5. Your Rights

5.1 Data Control Rights

  • Access: View all your todo items and expense records at any time
  • Modification: Edit or delete any record
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not designed for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you discover that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete the information.

7. Data Retention Period

Data Type Retention Period Notes
Todo items Permanent (until you delete) Stored locally on device
Expense records Permanent (until you delete) Stored locally on device
Audio recordings Deleted immediately after processing Not retained in cloud

8. Permissions Used

This App requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Used to record voice memos, which are converted to text and then deleted

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we will notify you through the app. We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically.
