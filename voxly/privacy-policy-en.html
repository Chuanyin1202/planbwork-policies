Voxly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

1. Overview

Welcome to Voxly Voice Mood Diary (hereinafter referred to as "Voxly" or "the App"). We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

By using Voxly, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Types of Information Collected

Personal Information:

  • Email address (for account registration and login)
  • Username or nickname (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Usage Information:

  • Voice recordings (stored locally on your device)
  • Transcribed text content (stored locally on your device)
  • Mood tags and emotion analysis results
  • Location information (optional, for recording mood locations)
  • App usage statistics (frequency, feature usage)

Technical Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Language settings
  • Time zone information
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Services: Voice-to-text transcription, emotion analysis, mood tracking
  • Personalized Experience: Provide personalized insights and suggestions based on your usage patterns
  • Data Backup: Provide cloud backup services (Pro version feature)
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to optimize app features
  • Technical Support: Resolve technical issues and provide customer service
  • Security Maintenance: Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your voice recordings, transcribed text, and personal mood records are stored locally on your device by default and are not automatically uploaded to the cloud. This ensures that your private content is completely under your control.

4.2 Cloud Backup (Pro Version)

If you choose to use the cloud backup feature:

  • Data is encrypted using TLS during transmission
  • Data is encrypted when stored in the cloud
  • Only you can access your backup data

4.3 Security Measures

  • App lock feature (PIN or biometric authentication)
  • Regular security updates
  • Following industry best security practices

5. Third-Party Services

Voxly uses the following third-party services to provide and improve functionality:

5.1 OpenAI Whisper API

  • Purpose: Voice-to-text transcription (Pro version cloud transcription)
  • Data Processing: Audio files are sent to OpenAI for processing
  • Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

5.2 Firebase

  • Purpose: Usage analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage
  • Data Processing: Anonymous usage statistics and technical data
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

5.3 Google Play / App Store

  • Purpose: App distribution and in-app purchase services
  • Data Processing: Transaction information and account data

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To provide services you have requested (such as cloud transcription)
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request export of your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Permanently stored on your device until manually deleted
  • Cloud Backup: Retained for 30 days after subscription cancellation
  • Account Data: Retained for 90 days after account deletion
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous statistics retained for 2 years

9. Children's Privacy

Voxly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us.

10. International Data Transfers

If you use our cloud services, your data may be transferred to servers outside your country/region for processing and storage. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Voxly constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@planbwork.com
  • In-App: Settings → Help & Support → Contact Us

We commit to responding to your inquiries within 7 business days.

13. Governing Law
